Clinic Management System

Role

  • Design & Development

    •

    A fully featured Clinic Management System having a well designed Database Schema.

    Built on 3 Tier Based Architecture

    01

    Presentation Tier

    The application tier manages the business logic and serves as an intermediary between the presentation and data tiers.
    In this project, this tier would handle the logic related to clinic operations, patient management, appointment scheduling, and other core functionalities.

    02

    Application Tier

    The application tier manages the business logic and serves as an intermediary between the presentation and data tiers.
    In this project, this tier would handle the logic related to clinic operations, patient management, appointment scheduling, and other core functionalities.S.

    03

    Data Tier

    The data tier is responsible for managing and storing the system's data.
    In this, this tier involves the database where patient records, appointment details, medical histories, and other relevant data are stored.

    Functionalities Implemented

    Patient Module

    The patient-oriented features of the system offer a comprehensive healthcare experience. Patients can seamlessly navigate their health journey through functionalities like viewing their profile, checking current appointments, and accessing complete bills and treatment histories. The 'Take Appointment' feature streamlines the appointment process by enabling patients to explore departments, select doctors, and request appointments with ease. Notifications keep patients informed about the status of their appointment requests, while the feedback option allows them to rate and provide insights after each appointment. Notably, patients are restricted to requesting only one appointment at a time, ensuring focused and manageable healthcare interactions until the current appointment is concluded.

    .

    Doctor Module

    The DoctorProfile module allows doctors to access and review their personal profiles, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information. In the PendingAppointments section, doctors can conveniently view all pending appointments associated with their unique doctor ID, facilitating efficient appointment management. The TodaysAppointments feature streamlines the daily schedule by presenting appointments scheduled for the current day, offering the flexibility to select or reject appointments. The HistoryUpdate functionality empowers doctors to update patient records, including prescriptions, disease details, and progress notes. Following the appointment, doctors can effortlessly generate bills using the GenerateBill feature. Lastly, the PatientHistory module provides doctors with a comprehensive overview of the treatment history for all patients they have attended to, fostering continuity of care and informed decision-making.

    .

    Admin Module

    The Admin Home serves as a comprehensive dashboard for administrators, providing a consolidated view of clinic statistics, including weekly appointments, clinic income, the number of registered patients and doctors, and a list of departments. Through the View Doctors, View Patients, and View Other Staff modules, administrators can access detailed information about registered doctors, patients, and other staff members, respectively, facilitating efficient management. The Search Box feature enables administrators to quickly locate specific employees within the clinic by name. Additionally, administrators possess the authority to dynamically manage clinic personnel through the Add/Remove functionality, allowing them to seamlessly add or remove doctors, patients, and other staff members as needed for effective clinic administration.
